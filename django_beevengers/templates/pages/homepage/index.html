About

This Website and accompanying Facebook page is for Alaska beekeepers (Alaska Beeks) and others seeking information about keeping honey bees, and maintaining honey bee health, in the 49th state. The honey-producing season in Alaska is brief, compared to seasons in other states, and after a mild spring and a wet and sunny summer, the calendar is rounded out by sometimes bone-chilling temperatures during our long winter months. This page is primarily intended as an encouragement for those involved and considering the art, informed by science, of beekeeping. This can become a welcoming question-and-answer forum for all of us. Most of all we want to encourage and help Alaska beeks be successful. Please join us in dialogs, questions and answers, about beekeeping in Alaska. We'd like to see this Website and Facebook page grow by means of a grassroots growth efforts!
